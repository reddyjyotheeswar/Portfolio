## KEY STRATEGIES FOR 3-MONTH NOTICE PERIOD

### 1. Early Application Strategy
- **Start applying immediately** - Don't wait until you're ready to leave
- **Be transparent about notice period** - Many companies will wait for strong candidates
- **Target companies with longer hiring processes** - MNCs, product companies

### 2. Salary Negotiation Leverage
- **Use notice period as strength** - Shows professionalism and commitment
- **Negotiate start date** - 3 months after acceptance
- **Sign-on bonus** - Request to offset notice period gap
- **Relocation package** - If moving cities

### 3. Risk Mitigation
- **Keep current job until offer is signed** - Don't resign prematurely
- **Have backup options** - Continue applying even after getting offers
- **Maintain good relations** - Don't burn bridges at Infosys
- **Financial buffer** - 3-6 months expenses saved

### 4. Timeline Acceleration
- **Express urgency** - Some companies can expedite hiring
- **Leverage referrals** - Can speed up process significantly
- **Be flexible** - Consider contract-to-hire if timeline is tight
- **Direct outreach** - Contact hiring managers directly

## NOTICE PERIOD BEST PRACTICES

### During Notice Period
- **Maintain professionalism** - Give 100% until last day
- **Document everything** - Create handover documentation
- **Train replacement** - Ensure smooth transition
- **Stay positive** - Don't complain about leaving
- **Network internally** - Keep good relationships

### Resignation Strategy
- **Resign in writing** - Formal resignation letter
- **Give exact notice** - 3 months as per policy
- **Offer help** - Propose transition plan
- **Be flexible** - Accommodate company needs
- **Plan exit** - Know final day and handover

### Exit Strategy
- **Complete all projects** - Leave no loose ends
- **Update documentation** - Ensure knowledge transfer
- **Return company property** - Clear all assets
- **Exit interview** - Provide constructive feedback
- **Stay in touch** - Maintain professional network

## CONTINGENCY PLANS

### If No Offers by Month 3
- **Extend job search** - Continue for additional 2-3 months
- **Upgrade skills** - Focus on in-demand technologies
- **Consider contract roles** - Build experience and network
- **Lower salary expectations** - Temporarily for better opportunities
- **Expand location** - Consider other cities/remote roles

### If Offer is Below Target Salary
- **Negotiate aggressively** - Use market data and skills
- **Consider total compensation** - Include benefits, bonus, stock
- **Growth potential** - Evaluate career progression
- **Company reputation** - Consider brand value
- **Backup plan** - Continue searching while negotiating

### If Current Company Counters
- **Evaluate counter offer** - Consider salary, role, growth
- **Know the risks** - 50% leave within 1 year anyway
- **Consider original reasons** - Why did you want to leave?
- **Negotiate if staying** - Get significant improvements
- **Make decision quickly** - Don't prolong uncertainty
